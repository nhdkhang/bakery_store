Our Story

Since 2025

Moonwalker Cake Shop, under a different name, began as a small family bakery in Ho Chi Minh City. Later on, the family business was shrunk down due to lack of resources, and the son with his friends decided to rebuilt again from scratch. What started as a passion for creating delicious, handcrafted cakes has grown into a beloved local institution known for our quality ingredients and creative designs.

Our Philosophy

We believe in baking with love, using only the finest ingredients, and creating memorable experiences one cake at a time. Every product we make carries our signature attention to detail and commitment to excellence.
